Hardman Photo is the home of Claire Hardman: Photographer and visual artist. Claire's background is in TV News and theatre amongst a host of other things. She focuses on the arts, documentary, portraits and anything that sounds like a challenging and exciting project. If you are looking for pictures that are one of a kind and breaking the mould then Claire Hardman is the thing for you. Claire is based in Stockholm and always loves to meet new people with new and inspiring ideas. For enquiries, love letters and hate mail just send it through to: contact@hardmanphoto.se
